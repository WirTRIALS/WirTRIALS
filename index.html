About Us

We are a company based in the industrious city of Chemnitz founded by the students of Chemnitz University of Technology in 2020. We strive to develop unmatched IT solutions for the most challenging problems. Idea too unrealistic? Here in WirTRIALS, we will make your dream turn into reality by providing the most cutting-edge solutions to your problems.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to achieve a connection between concepts or ideas and the real world by developing tools which are custom built, simple and user friendly. We are highly eager in providing quick and full-proof solutions or support to our customers.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to support our clients to excel in new technologies with the help of our IT solutions and services, and thereby going hand in hand to forge the digital future.
